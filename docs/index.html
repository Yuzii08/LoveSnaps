Features
Everything for you two
📸

Daily Snaps

Send spontaneous polaroid-style snaps to each other. No followers, no likes — just you two.

🔥

Love Streaks

Tap each day to keep your love streak alive. Miss a day and it resets — a gentle reminder to stay connected.

📍

Live Distance

Always know how far away your partner is with real-time location sharing. No more "where are you?" texts.

💝

Days Counter

Track the exact number of days you've been together. Watch your love story grow with every sunrise.

🔔

Miss You Pings

Send a gentle "Miss You" notification with one tap. Let them know you're thinking of them, instantly.

🔒

Just You Two

No public profiles, no strangers. LoveSnaps is an exclusive space only accessible by the two of you.
